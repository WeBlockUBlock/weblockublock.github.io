WeBlockU-Block finds new ways to prevent uBlock (and any other adblockers) from blocking your promotional material.

Why does this site exist

Not too long ago uBlock Origin - an open-source ad and content blocker - added a change that targets and removes "ads" and sponsors from project pages on Content-sharing sites like Modrinth and Curseforge. This change was reverted due to countless false-positives and site-breaking changes.

uBlock's team members have been rude to us, deleting comments of genuine concern and questions from mod devs and calling us a "moronic team".

What uBlock does, most see as justified. Blocking dynamic ads is one thing, but attempting to block sponsors on a project page is another. Modders put countless hours into their mods often without getting any monetary compensation in the end. Sponsors are a good way for them to earn something from their hard work, without putting it behind a form of pay/donation-wall. Blocking these sponsors means blocking a modders financial help.
